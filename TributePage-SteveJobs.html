STEVE JOBS

(1955 - 2011)

Steven Paul Jobs was the chairman, chief executive officer(CEO),and co-founder of Apple Inc.Apple's revolutionary products, which include the iPod, iPhone and iPad, are now seen as dictating the evolution of modern technology.

Steve Jobs was co-founder of Apple Inc.

The following list is a time line of Steve Jobs Life
  • 1955 - born in San Francisco, Callifornie.
  • 1973 - attend but later drops out of Reed College.
  • 1975 - Steve and Woz assemble Apple I computer
  • 1976 - founding Apple Inc.
  • 1995 - Job's left Apple and started a new company firm NexT
  • 1986 - buys Pixar Animation Studio
  • 1991 - marries Laurene Powel
  • 2000 - Steve is back as CEO at Apple Inc.
  • 2009 - Jobs take medical leave for six months and has surgery for liver transplant
  • 2010 - introduces iPad, iPhone 4 and iPod Touch 4G and new Macbook Air
  • 2011 - resigned his position as CEO of Apple
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs passes away
